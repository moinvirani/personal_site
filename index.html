My Journey So Far

I was born in Mumbai, India and raised in Dubai, UAE where I spent most of my early years. At the age of 16 I moved to Atlanta, Georgia where I completed my highschool and later moved to Ottawa, Ontario to complete my undergraduate degree.

I believe change is inevitible and I have learnt this early in my life. I have been fortunate enough to travel to a dozen countries where I learnt about various cultures and global viewpoints. I truly believe in the saying that a mans work defines his worth, which is why I constantly strive for the best and am always critical of the status quo.
